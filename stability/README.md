Intact BH3 motif is required by BIK, BID, BAK, BAD and BAX for their pro-apoptotic activity and for their interaction with anti-apoptotic members of the Bcl-2 family.

So the list (BIK, BID, BAK, BAD, BAX) is examples of proteins that share this BH3 motif, not components of BAK.

In short: BAK is one protein; the others are separate proteins that share similar BH3-dependent function.
